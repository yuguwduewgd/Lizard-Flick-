Lizard Flick is a vibrant, reflex-based game where speed and precision determine your score. Flick a lively lizard across the screen, rack up points, and compete against the clock in colorful, animated environments!

## Features

- **Moving Background:** Enjoy dynamic, animated backgrounds that change as you progress, creating a more immersive and visually engaging experience. The scenery smoothly scrolls or shifts, making every match feel lively and fresh.
- **Intuitive Controls:** Simple drag/swipe actions let you flick the lizard with ease.
- **Increasing Difficulty:** Levels become faster and introduce new obstacles as you improve.
- **Score Tracking:** Keep track of your best runs and challenge your reflexes!

## Getting Started

## Gameplay

- Flick the lizard by swiping or dragging.
- Aim to collect points and avoid moving obstacles.
- The moving background changes with progress, adding excitement and challenge.
- Try to beat your previous high scores!
